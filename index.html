Who We Are

IEPC Church is a Burundian community church located in Elizabeth, opposite TAFE. We are a Christian church that believes in Jesus Christ, the Son of God, our Redeemer and Saviour.

We believe that Jesus Christ died for our sins so that we may be saved. Through Him, we have forgiveness, hope, restoration, and eternal life.

Where to Find Us

We welcome everyone to join us for worship, prayer, fellowship, and the teaching of the Word of God.
